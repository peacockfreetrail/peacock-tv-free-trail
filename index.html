How to Get Your Peacock TV Free Trial in 2025

As of 2025, Peacock TV continues to offer free trial periods for new subscribers. Here's how you can get yours:

1Check Current Offers

Visit the official Peacock TV website and look for current promotional offers. These are typically prominently displayed on their homepage.

2Create an Account

Sign up for a new account using your email address. You'll need to provide basic information and create a password.

3Select Premium Plan

Choose either the Premium or Premium Plus plan. Both typically qualify for free trial periods, with Premium Plus offering ad-free viewing.

4Enter Payment Information

You'll need to provide a valid payment method. Your card won't be charged until after the trial period ends.

5Start Streaming

Once confirmed, you can immediately start enjoying Peacock TV's premium content library for the duration of your trial.

Pro Tip

Set a calendar reminder for 2 days before your trial ends. This gives you time to decide whether to continue with the paid subscription or cancel without charges.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the Peacock TV free trial?

As of 2025, Peacock typically offers a 7-day free trial for new subscribers, though occasionally they run promotions with extended trial periods.

Can I get another free trial if I've had one before?

Generally, free trials are limited to one per account. However, using a different email address and payment method may allow you to access another trial.

What happens if I forget to cancel my trial?

If you don't cancel before the trial period ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid plan, and your payment method will be charged.

Is there a completely free version of Peacock TV?

Yes, Peacock offers a limited free tier with ads and a smaller content library. The free trial gives you temporary access to the premium content without payment.
